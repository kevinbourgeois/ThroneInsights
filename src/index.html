THRONES INSIGHTS

The context of using this dataset will help explain the trend in the number of viewers at the first airing of each episode of the Game of Thrones series.

WHAT IS GAME OF THRONES ?

Game of Thrones is a medieval fantasy television series based on the novels by George R.R. Martin. It follows the power struggles between several noble families in a fictional world called Westeros. The series is known for its intricate plot, graphic violence, unexpected twists and turns and many memorable characters. Over the course of the seasons, the characters face deadly enemies, political plots and supernatural threats as they struggle to conquer the Iron Throne and rule the seven kingdoms of Westeros.

How to properly read our graph

Through this graph you will be able to dive back into the history of the greatest series of all time. Scroll from episode to episode remembering the great moments of Game Of Thrones.

Each emoji represents an event in the episode that we considered important or a key event. To make it easier for you to use the graph that follows, below, in the section you are currently reading, there are captions that indicate what each emoji means.

When you click on an episode with a normal ray, you will have access to the details of the episode with a summary to read. For the episodes with a bigger radius, in addition to the details, they contain an insight.

What does the insight represent?

It is our personal opinion, after analysis, on the reasons for a strong decrease or increase of the audience. Whether it is directly related to the show itself or to events that are not related to it.
